About Me


Who made this website?

Hey! I'm Aniruddha Murali, and I'm a 19 year-old freshman at Georgia Tech studying computer science.


Why did I make this website?

After seeing how widespread and devastating the impact from COVID-19 has been, I wanted to see if there was anything I could do to help people out. Recently, I've been learning a lot about data visualization and web development, and I wanted to see if I could make something that people would find useful in these difficult times. My goal with this website was to provide real-time information on COVID-19 so people can stay up-to-date. I hope you're finding it useful!
